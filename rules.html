⚔️ 「 LightCitySMP — Constitution 」 ⚔️

✤ ═══════════════════════════ ✤

LightCitySMP runs on one principle — there are rules, and they exist for a reason. This isn't a casual server where anything goes. Every law below was put in place to make sure the SMP stays competitive, fair, and worth playing on. Ignorance is not an excuse.

📢

The High Council reserves the right to update, add, or remove laws at any time. It is your responsibility to stay informed.

・
⚔️ » PvP ›
  • Using tridents or elytras in a 1v1 is prohibited, but it is allowed in a 1v2s+
  • Elytra macing using fireworks is prohibited, but elytra macing normally is allowed.
  • If you're fighting someone, and you manage to get out of combat timer, you aren't allowed to do /home, /team home, /rtp, /tpa, /spawn or any command to escape unless you're too far away and your opponent doesn't know where you are.
  • Using pearl chambers is strictly prohibited when dueling or fighting, but is allowed for quick transportation.
  • Naked killing is also strictly prohibited and COULD get you permanently banned from the SMP.
  • TP trapping / TP killing is allowed.
  • TNT, TNT minecarts and beds are not allowed in PvP, but can be used for trapping.
✤
🏗️ » Building & Griefing ›
  • Destroying or blowing up bases is strictly prohibited.
  • Raiding bases is allowed.
  • Bases below Y -50 are considered illegal and are not allowed.
✤
🤝 » Alliances & Wars ›
  • Alliances are only allowed for either trading or having a non-aggression pact but are not allowed for teaming up on teams. For example: 2 teams (allied) vs 1 team is strictly prohibited. We only allow 1 team vs 1 team.
  • We suggest players to fight their wars in the desert so they don't destroy and damage the nature of LightCitySMP.
  • Killing villagers or animals is prohibited, only ones that are owned (which means that are in other's bases). Stealing them is allowed.
✤
🛡️ » Cheating & Exploiting ›
  • ESP, Xray, seed cracking, abusing bugs and abusing glitches are extremely prohibited and will lead to a long-term ban.
  • Any kind of cheating client is banned. Anyone that is caught using them will be permanently banned off of the SMP.
  • Banned mods: Health indicator (or any mod that sees the other player's HP), minimaps that let you see players, mobs, etc.
✤
📜 » General Conduct ›
  • Toxicity is allowed but has its limits. Don't go too far.
  • If you don't have fun, then create it.
