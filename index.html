All 18+ Abilities

Ascension I — Low Power

  • Iron Skin: Resistance I for 8s. CD: 40s.
  • Dash: Forward launch. CD: 15s.
  • Smokescreen: Blinds target 3s. CD: 45s.

Ascension II — Low Power

  • Blood Rush: Speed II on hit 5s. CD: 45s.
  • Fortify: -25% damage 8s on hit. CD: 50s.
  • Splinter: +8% damage 8s. CD: 50s.

Ascension III — Medium

  • Exposed: +10% dmg to target 4s. CD: 60s.
  • Phantom Step: Invis 4s. CD: 65s.
  • Shockwave: Knockback 5b, 2 hearts. CD: 55s.

Ascension IV — Medium

  • Overclock: Speed III + Haste II 30s. CD: 120s.
  • Soul Drain: Strip target effects. CD: 75s.
  • Warden's Grasp: Root 3s + Slow X 5s. CD: 500s.

Ascension V — High

  • Wraith Form: Invis + no damage 5s. CD: 150s.
  • Execution: 2x dmg below 25% HP. CD: 85s.
  • Marked: +15% dmg 5s. CD: 95s.

Ascension VI — High

  • Soul Lock: Silence + freeze. CD: 750s.
  • Warlord's Aura: Str III + Speed III 8s. CD: 300s.
  • Reaper's Call: Blind + nausea 4s. CD: 800s.

Kill Streaks

Every 5 kill streak = 1 Rebirth Point. Dying resets streak to 0.

Display

  • 5–14 — orange tag
  • 15–24 — purple tag
  • 25–30 — blood red, larger
×

Plugin Information

Ascension is a PvP & grinding plugin where players earn Rebirth Points through kill streaks or crafting, unlocking abilities. With each rebirth, abilities grow stronger.
