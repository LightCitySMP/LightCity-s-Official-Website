⚔️ About Staff Weapons

Staff Weapons are special melee weapons that grow in power as you unlock additional Forms. Each weapon starts at Form 1 and can be upgraded up to Form 3, unlocking new passive effects and increased damage with each tier.

💡 How Forms Work

Forms are unlocked by meeting specific in-game requirements (usually kill counts or achievements). Once a Form is unlocked, its passive buff is permanently active — you don't need to switch between them. Higher Forms stack their benefits on top of lower ones.

🚀 Getting Started

📋 Create a Clan

Open everything from the /clan menu — no subcommands, it's all in the GUI. Founding a clan costs 15 renown. Click Create Clan and type a name (5–16 characters).

💬 Clan Chat

Talk privately to your clan with /cc or /clanchat. Toggle it on/off, or use /cc <message> for a single message.

👑 Leader Perks

Clan leaders earn passive income while online, scaling with clan tier. Reaching Tier 3 also grants the temporary [LORD] rank.

👥 Clan Roles

Role Powers & Responsibilities
👑 Leader Full control: invite, promote/demote, kick, rename, set join requirement, set sigil, disband, and transfer leadership. Earns clan passive income.
⚔️ Officer Can invite players and manage members (kick/promote below their rank).
🛡️ Member Contributes to clan renown, completes quests, and helps push tier progress.

🏆 Lord Rank

Reaching Tier 3 makes the leader a temporary [LORD] — a special server rank kept for as long as they remain the clan leader.

🎯 How to Rank Up

Tiers unlock automatically once every member individually meets all requirements. No manual application needed — the system checks and promotes when ready.

Tier 1 Unlock

Each member must complete:

  • 5 distinct player kills
  • 24 hours total playtime

Tier 2 Unlock

Each member must complete:

  • 10 distinct player kills
  • 48 hours total playtime
  • Fully Netherite achievement

Tier 3 Unlock

Each member must complete:

  • 15 distinct player kills
  • 100 hours total playtime
  • Beacon achievement

ℹ️ Distinct Kills Explained

"Distinct kills" means different victims — repeatedly killing the same player doesn't count. There is a 1-hour cooldown on counting kills against the same victim.

⚡ Renown System

Renown is your clan's reputation and the key to its power. It determines your standing on the leaderboard and unlocks clan features.

⚠️ Healthy Clan Requirement

A clan only earns renown from its members' kills while it is "healthy":

  • The leader must have at least 10 renown
  • Every member must have at least 5 renown

Keep your clan active or your renown income stops entirely.

💰 Clan Leader Income

While the leader is online, the clan pays them automatically on a set timer. The amount and frequency scale with tier.

Tier 0 3 LC every 30 min 64 gold every 40 min
Tier 1 6 LC every 30 min 128 gold every 40 min

Tier 3 16 LC every 45 min 650 gold every 60 min

📜 Clan Quests

Quests unlock at Tier 1. Only one active quest at a time, with a 1-hour cooldown after completing one. On completion, the clan gains renown and every online member gets Light Orbs (LO).

🔧 Clan Features

🏠 Clan Homes

Shared teleport locations for the whole clan. 1 home unlocks at Tier 1, and a 2nd home unlocks at Tier 3.

🎨 Clan Sigil

Available at Tier 2+. Set a custom banner as your clan's emblem by holding a banner and using the sigil command in the clan menu.

🔒 Join Requirement

Available at Tier 1+. Leaders can set a minimum personal renown that players must have to join the clan.

📋 Members

View the full clan roster, check individual member stats, and manage roles directly from the clan GUI.

🤝 Allies

Browse every clan on the server, sorted by renown. View each clan's owner and shared renown to consider allying.

⚔️ Enemies

Browse every clan on the server, sorted by renown. View each clan's owner and shared renown to declare war.

📋 Quick Command Reference

Command Description
/clan Open the main clan GUI — all features are here
/cc / /clanchat Toggle clan chat mode, or send a single message
/cc <message> Send one message to clan chat without toggling

💡 Tip

There are no subcommands for clans — everything from creating, inviting, managing tiers, setting sigils, and browsing allies/enemies is done through the /clan GUI menu.
